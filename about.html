About


Shenandoah Valley Pride Alliance, Inc. is a 501c3 nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting understanding of the LGBTQ community through outreach and education. Our mission includes providing education on LGBTQ issues through sponsored presentations and outreach efforts throughout the year. Additionally, we host an annual Pride Festival event in the summer, bringing the community together to foster greater understanding of the LGBTQ community.

Meet Our Team

President: Russell Love

Russell, a native Floridian, relocated to the Shenandoah Valley Area in 2013 with his husband, Philip. They reside in Staunton with three beloved dogs. Russell's experience with non-profits in Florida has equipped him with valuable skills in organization and time management. He serves as our President/Executive Director.

Vice President: Natalie Gazzara

Natalie recently joined the board and brings with her several years of working with non-profits and serving the community both professionally and through volunteering. She has lived in western Rockingham County most of her life and currently resides in Mt. Crawford with her wife, son, and two dogs. In her spare time, she enjoys spending time with friends and family, listening to music, and reading.

Treasurer: Michele Sullivan

Michele, a native New Yorker, settled in the Valley in 2013. With a passion for community service, she served as President of the Board of Directors for the renamed Center in Orlando. Michele is actively involved in local organizations, leads two employee resource groups at Merck, and plays a crucial role in SVPA's policy formation and leadership preparation.
