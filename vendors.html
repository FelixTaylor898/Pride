Become a vendor at Shenandoah Valley Pride

2025 Vendor Registration

Businesses and organizations who register before September 1st, 2024 to be a vendor and send in payment in a timely manner will be allowed use of one (1) 10' x 10' space on Court Square or the surrounding area for the duration of the Shenandoah Valley Pride Festival.

Please bring your own canopy, tables, and chairs.

The event will proceed rain or shine, so please plan accordingly for the summer weather. We apologize, but we cannot guarantee access to electricity for any vendors. If you require electricity, it is recommended to bring a generator.

Register: Click the "Register" button below and fill in the form with your organization's details, then proceed to the payment portion to confirm your registration.

Payment: Vendor registration is priced at $100, while non-profit registration is $25. Please note that registration is not considered complete until payment is received. Kindly be aware that registration fees are non-refundable. Kindly click on the button below to make your online payment and finalize your registration. In the "Note" box, please leave a comment to let us know who you are.
